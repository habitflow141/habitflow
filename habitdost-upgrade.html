Common questions

Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. You can cancel anytime from Settings → Your Plan. Your Pro access continues until your subscription end date — we don't cut you off early. No refunds are issued after 7 days of purchase.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
All major Indian payment methods — UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), debit cards, credit cards, and net banking. Powered by Razorpay.
Will my data be safe if I downgrade? ▼
Yes. All your habits and streak history are always saved. If you downgrade to free, you just lose access to Pro features — your data stays intact.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
We offer a full refund within 7 days of purchase — no questions asked. After 7 days, refunds are not available but you can still cancel and keep Pro access until your subscription period ends. Contact us at habitflow141@gmail.com for refund requests.
What happens when I delete my account? ▼
Your account is soft-deleted — your data (habits, streaks, logs) is kept safely for 30 days before being permanently removed. If you deleted by mistake, contact us at habitflow141@gmail.com within 30 days and we'll fully restore your account. After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
