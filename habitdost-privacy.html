Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. HabitDost collects only what is necessary to provide you with the best habit tracking experience. We do not sell your data to anyone.

1. Information We Collect

When you use HabitDost, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up
  • Habit data — habits you create, daily check-ins, streaks, and logs
  • Payment information — payment status and plan details (we do not store card numbers — payments are processed by Razorpay)
  • Device information — basic browser and device type for improving performance

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the HabitDost service
  • Send you reminders and streak alerts (only if you enable them)
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Protect the security of your account

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing
  • Supabase — for secure data storage
  • Firebase — for push notification delivery
  • When required by law or to protect the rights of HabitDost

5. Cookies

HabitDost uses minimal cookies and local storage only to keep you logged in and remember your theme preference. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Danger Zone. When you delete your account, your data is kept for 30 days before being permanently removed. If you change your mind, contact us within 30 days at habitflow141@gmail.com to restore your account.

7. Children's Privacy

HabitDost is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of HabitDost after changes means you accept the updated policy.
