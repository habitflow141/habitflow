❓ Common Questions

💰 How do I get a refund? ▼
We offer a full refund within 7 days of purchase. Fill the form above, select "Refund Request" as the topic, and include your Transaction ID. We'll process it within 2 business days.
✨ My Pro didn't activate ▼
After payment, your Pro plan activates automatically within a few seconds. Try refreshing the page. If it still shows Free, contact us with your Transaction ID and we'll fix it manually.
💳 My payment failed ▼
If your payment failed, no amount is deducted. Try again using a different UPI app or payment method. If amount was deducted but plan didn't activate, contact us immediately with your Transaction ID.
❌ How do I delete my account? ▼
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. Your data is kept for 30 days before permanent deletion. Contact us within 30 days to restore your account if needed.
